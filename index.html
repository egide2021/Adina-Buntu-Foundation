🕊️ Adina Buntu Foundation

Inspired by a miracle. Driven by hope.

🌟 Welcome Message

Born at just 25 weeks and weighing 1.36 pounds, Adina's life began with a fierce fight for survival. Inspired by her miraculous journey, this foundation was born to offer hope, compassion, and practical support to NICU families around the world. You are not alone—we walk with you through the storm, believing in the light that follows.

🎯 Mission Statement

To support families with premature and critically ill newborns in Neonatal Intensive Care Units (NICUs) through faith-based encouragement, financial assistance, and community support.

👁️ Vision Statement

A world where every NICU family receives the hope, help, and healing they need, regardless of background or means.

💖 Core Values

  • Faith – Trusting God in every step of the journey.
  • Compassion – Loving and standing with families in crisis.
  • Hope – Believing in miracles and brighter tomorrows.
  • Integrity – Serving with transparency and honesty.
  • Community – Uniting people to lift others in need.

🤝 What We Do

  • Offer emotional and spiritual support to families with NICU babies
  • Provide small financial grants to help with medical-related expenses
  • Raise awareness about premature birth and NICU care
  • Share real stories of hope, healing, and perseverance

📢 Call to Action

Your support can help save a tiny life. Join us in bringing light to NICU families through your prayers, donations, or volunteering. Every gift—small or large—makes a lasting difference.
